BMW, or Bayerische Motoren Werke, is a German luxury car brand known for combining performance, innovation, and elegant design


The core components of a BMW include its powerful engine, like the famous inline-six or V8 twin-turbo, paired with a ZF 8-speed automatic transmission for smooth shifting.
Most models feature BMW's iDrive infotainment system, advanced driver-assist tech, and the signature kidney grille. The brand also focuses heavily on handling through its near 50:50 weight distribution and rear-wheel-drive or xDrive all-wheel-drive systems. BMW offers several model types to fit different needs: the 3 Series and 5 Series sedans for executive luxury, the X Series like X3 and X5 for SUVs, the sporty Z4 roadster, and the high-performance M Series like the M3 and M5 built for track-level power. Recently, BMW expanded into electric vehicles with the i Series, including the i4 sedan and iX SUV, showing its commitment to sustainable performance.
